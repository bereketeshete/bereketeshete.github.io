  • Terminologies

    • Machine Learning: When a computer acquires the ability to solve a task through examples, rather than human-encoded of handcrafted rules. (Deep Learning with PyTorch, Eli stevens)
    • Transfer Learning

    • Deep learning: a technique that leverages large amounts of data to approximate complex functions whose inputs and outputs are far apart. (Deep Learning with PyTorch, Eli stevens)

    • Aritifical Neural Network: a sequence of layers that each perform simple operation [38]

    • Convolutional layer: utilizing spatial information by applying fitlers to patches of neighbouhood pixels [38]
    • Residual Neural Network: a network that links past layers to present layers by skip connections to avoid the vanishing gradient problem [38]
